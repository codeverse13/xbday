Our Story

It all began on a soft February 23rd. That day, neither of us said it out loud but deep inside we had already tucked a little piece of each other into our hearts. We weren’t lovers yet but we were something — something gentle and growing. We started as friends, the kind that find comfort in every conversation, in every shared meme, every "are you okay?" and "don’t forget to eat."

Days passed and friendship bloomed into best-friendship. We became each other’s diary, secret keeper, and silent cheerleader. We knew each other’s chaos, the curves of every mood, every scar, every beautiful flaw. In vacations when the world slowed down our bond grew faster. We were just two souls discovering that no matter the distance or silence we’d always return to each other.

It was in the stolen moments brushing shoulders, resting heads, that warm silence while watching something together where love took root. From quick handholds under the table to long deep stares we never spoke of, we were falling. And when the fall became too loud to hide, we still whispered it.

There were late night confessions, sweet jealousy, us figuring out how to navigate a world that wasn’t ready for us just yet. We kept our love hidden, tucked behind friend labels and quiet glances. But inside we were already "us." We were already dreaming together.

Slowly we unfolded in public. What was once a secret bloomed under the sun. We had our first little date shy smiles, nervous laughter, yet everything felt so right. From hiding behind walls to holding hands proudly, from teasing nicknames to forehead kisses, we grew. We weren’t rushing. We were blooming.

Every moment became a memory. The way your fingers found mine like they belonged there. The sound of your sleepy "goodnight" like a lullaby only I got to hear. The comfort of your presence when my mind was too loud. From fights to makeups, from misunderstandings to midnight hugs, we built something real — a love that doesn’t flinch.

Then came December. On the 3rd of December, we finally said what was always written between our lines. We finally got together. It wasn’t sudden it was inevitable. That moment sealed the truth we always carried that we were meant to be. That day, everything aligned and our love became real in every possible way.

Now here we are. From friends to best friends, from almosts to always. We’ve made it through storms, through silence, through days when words failed but love didn’t. We know each other’s curves the mood swings, the chaos, the tenderness, and the kind of love that walks in even when you push it away. We know us. And we choose us, again and again.

Ours is not just a love story. It’s a universe. A collage of warmth, truth, softness, and fire. And the best part? We’re just getting started. And this little gift this whole page is here to remind us of all of that. Because today is 2nd July, your birthday, and if this makes you even smile a little, then its purpose is already fulfilled.
