21 Wishes for the Love of My Life

I wish for you to wake up every morning knowing you are deeply loved

to look in the mirror and see the magic I see in you

to walk through your days with grace in your steps and courage in your chest

to always feel me right beside you, in silence or storm

to never doubt your worth, even when the world feels too heavy

and to know you are my forever kind of person

I wish you never stop smiling like you do when you’re sleepy and soft

because that smile holds galaxies I could get lost in

I wish the world slows down when you're overwhelmed

so you can breathe and remember how strong you are

and I hope you always come back to my arms when it all feels too much

because I’ll always be your safest place

I wish the universe gifts you peace every time your heart aches

and fills your chest with warmth when the night feels too long

I hope you always have music that feels like your heartbeat

and people who see you like I do the whole constellation

I wish you find beauty in the quiet things

and in moments you didn’t even know were special until later

I wish you never forget the way I look at you

like you're the only miracle that ever mattered

like the world built itself just to hold your softness

I wish every sunset reminds you of our little forever

and every breeze feels like a whisper from me

saying you are loved beyond every language

I wish the future never scares you too much

because you carry light in your hands and bravery in your spine

I hope you chase your dreams like they’ve been waiting for you

and find me clapping at every single milestone

I wish you always believe in the fire you hold

and know I believe in you endlessly

I wish you never run out of words to say to yourself

especially the kind that remind you how lovely you are

how human and healing and worthy

how every scar on you still sings

I hope you speak gently to your own soul

and never forget your voice can move mountains

I wish your laughter never fades into silence

and your joy never feels too loud for this world

I hope you dance like you used to when you were little

unafraid of how much space you take up

I hope I can always be the one clapping offbeat

just happy you’re smiling

I wish you keep falling in love with life again and again

with the tiny details that most people miss

the sound of birds, the light through your lashes

your name in my mouth on a quiet morning

you are poetry in motion, baby

and I’ll keep rereading you forever

I wish the world is kind to your softness

that it doesn’t take advantage of your heart

that the people around you protect you like I do

with love like armor and gentleness like fire

you deserve safety in every form

and I will always be part of that

I wish you always feel beautiful in your own skin

even on days when the mirror lies

I hope you see what I see in you

a soul that radiates gold and grace

I wish you feel loved by your reflection

and adored by the universe itself

I wish your mind feels safe inside itself

with no storms louder than your peace

I hope your thoughts feel like warm hands

and your overthinking rests in my love

I wish I could live in your mind some days

just to leave little love notes behind

I wish your hands never feel empty

that they’re always filled with purpose or mine

I hope they get to create beautiful things

painted joy, written love, soft gestures

I wish to hold them in my sleep and my waking

until forever forgets how to count

I wish every tear you ever cry brings healing

not shame or silence

but space to feel and space to grow

and I hope I’m always the one who wipes them

who says nothing but stays anyway

loving you through the ache

I wish for you a life filled with tiny joys

like the perfect bite of cake or your favorite song in traffic

like finding someone has already warmed your spot in bed

or someone remembering how you take your tea

I want to be that someone

in every lifetime we’re given

I wish your name feels like a blessing to your own ears

not something to hide or apologize for

but something that blooms when whispered

I will always say your name like a prayer

like a song that saved me

because it did

I wish your past never haunts you

but reminds you how much you've grown

I hope the shadows stay behind you

and the future stays soft

I’ll walk beside you through both

hand in hand, always

I wish your laugh never forgets its way out

even when you feel too tired to smile

I want to be the reason it escapes again

even just once on a hard day

your laughter is my favorite music

and I never want it to fade

I wish love always feels like this for you

safe and warm and a little wild

unfolding in everyday moments

not just fireworks but the candle that stays lit

and if this is the love you asked for

I hope I get to keep giving it to you

I wish your dreams come true

the quiet ones and the loud ones

the ones you speak and the ones you hide

I wish you always feel deserving of joy

and that joy always leads you back to me

because you’re the heart of mine

I wish you always remember this day

as a celebration of you and everything you are

a girl who loves deeply and fights silently

who carries both fire and softness like art

I wish to celebrate you every day

because loving you feels like a lifetime gift

I wish you know this — you are my heart

you are the softness in my hard days

the home I never thought I’d find

you are the reason behind every smile I wear

and today isn’t just your birthday

it’s the day the world gave me my miracle
